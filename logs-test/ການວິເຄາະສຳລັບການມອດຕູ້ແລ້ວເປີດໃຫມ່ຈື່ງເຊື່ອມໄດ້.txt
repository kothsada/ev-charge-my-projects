  Root cause: Charger firmware freeze (zombie state)
  
  The charger's OCPP communication layer (WebSocket, Heartbeat,
  StatusNotification) was running fine — it appeared ONLINE to the server.
  But the charging management layer (the part that processes RemoteStart
  and starts physical charging) was frozen internally.

  The OCPP service sent RemoteStart over the live WebSocket but the
  charger's firmware never processed it, so no CALLRESULT came back within
  15 seconds → TIMEOUT.

  A physical restart reinitializes the firmware completely → RemoteStart
  works again.
